The langoLAB Virtual Dual Immersion Platform

Is your school interested in adding a virtual immersion component to its language curriculum?

Langolab lets you introduce a virtual language exchange program into your department the easy way.

With Langolab, students get paired with native speakers of the target language to converse and interact in real time. Virtual dual immersion accelerates acquisition, increases oral proficiency, motivates learners, and facilitates authentic intercultural exploration.

  • We take care of finding tandem partners for your students. Our scheduling system makes it easy for students to reserve time with each other.
  • While typing to each other, Langolab provides tools to help with vocab and grammar, using artificial intelligence to link conversational content back to classroom lessons.
  • Our teacher dashboard makes it easy for teachers to grade students' interactions.
